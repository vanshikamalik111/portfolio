VEG MOMOS: The very authentic taste of Himachali Momos...

Ingredeints Required

  • White Flour
  • cabbage
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • sweet corn
  • cheese

Procedure

  1. Take whie flour and mix 1 cup of water such that a fine dough can be prepared.
  2. Make sure that the dough you have made is neither too tight nor too smooth
  3. let it settle for 10-15 mins

    4. For Stuffing

  4. Take finely choped garlic and saute it for a while
  5. Add finely choped cabbage,sweet corn,cheese and cook it for 10 mins
  6. add salt as per taste,followed by 2 teaspoon of black pepper
  7. To enhance the taste. Add oregano and red chilli flakes
  8. Your stuffing is ready
  9. Take small portion of dough and roll into a small roti and fill the stuffing
  10. now make the shape as shown in the image
  11. Your momos are ready to steam.Steam it for 20min
  12. your momos are ready to serve

ENJOY!!
